# Godville Heroic Moment

***

A simple browser extension that adds a touch of humor and epicness to your Godville experience. This extension generates a "Heroic Moment" block on your hero's page, displaying a unique image and a witty quote generated by AI based on the latest adventures in their diary.

### Features

***

*   **Dynamic Generation**: Creates a "Heroic Moment" block that updates periodically.
*   **AI-Powered Images**: Generates a unique image based on your hero's recent actions, equipment, and pet, used the amazing Pollinations.ai api.
*   **Humorous Quotes**: Creates a quote with Godville's characteristic sarcastic and witty humor to accompany the image.
*   **Seamless Integration**: The new block visually integrates with the Godville page design.

### Installation

***

This extension is designed for **Firefox**.

1.  Download the `.xpi` file.
2.  Open Firefox and type `about:addons` in the address bar.
3.  Drag and drop the downloaded `.xpi` file onto the add-ons page.
4.  Accept the permissions to complete the installation.

### Usage

***

Once installed, the extension works automatically. Simply visit your superhero's page in Godville, and you will see the "Heroic Moment" block appear. The content will update on its own from time to time to reflect your hero's new feats (or blunders).
